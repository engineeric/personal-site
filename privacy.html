Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important. This website does not collect personal information such as your name, email, or contact details. However, it does use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site.


Google Analytics

Google Analytics is a web analytics service provided by Google. It uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and analyze information about how visitors interact with the site. This includes data such as pages visited, time spent on pages, and the type of device or browser used.

The information collected is aggregated and does not personally identify you. Data may be shared with other Google services and used to improve how the site is presented and accessed.

You can learn more about how Google handles data by visiting Google’s Privacy & Terms.


Your Choices

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. You can also disable cookies in your browser settings.
